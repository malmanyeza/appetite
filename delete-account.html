Account & Data Deletion

At Appetite, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated information, please follow the instructions below.

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose access to your order history, delivery addresses, and driver records (if applicable).

How to request deletion

You can request account deletion by sending an email from the address associated with your account to our privacy team. We will process your request and delete your information from our active databases within 30 days.

Send Deletion Request Email

What data will be deleted?

  • Profile information (Name, Email, Phone Number).
  • Saved delivery addresses.
  • Order history and receipts.
  • Driver profile and verification documents (if applicable).

Data Retention

Please note that some transaction records may be retained for a limited period where required by law (e.g., for tax or accounting purposes) or to prevent fraud. This data will be securely archived and will not be used for any other purpose.
