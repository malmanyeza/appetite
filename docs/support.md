## 🍽️ Customer FAQ

### How do I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you will automatically be navigated to the Order Tracking page. You can follow your driver in real-time as they approach your delivery location.

### My driver hasn't moved yet?
Drivers must first navigate to the restaurant and wait for the food to be prepared before heading your way. Real-time GPS movement usually starts once the status changes to "On the way".

### How do I switch to a Driver Account?
If you've been approved as a driver, open your Account Settings from the main menu and use the **Switch to Driver** button. 

---

## 🛵 Driver FAQ

### I can't accept an order?
Ensure your connection is stable and that you have switched your status to **Online** via the toggle switch in the top right corner of the Jobs dashboard.

### How do I update my profile picture?
Currently, profile pictures and licenses are uploaded during initial onboarding. If you need to update your license photos, please email our support team directly.
