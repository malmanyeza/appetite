Request Account Deletion

At Appetite, we strictly respect your privacy and provide a clear, easy way for you to permanently delete your account and associated personal data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account using either of the following methods:

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended & Instant)

  1. Open the Appetite app on your device.
  2. Log in to your account.
  3. Navigate to your Profile Settings (or tapping your avatar icon).
  4. Tap on Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your decision. Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Method 2: Email Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us directly:

Send an email to: malmanyeza@gmail.com
Subject Line: "Account Deletion Request"
Body: Please include the email address or phone number associated with your Appetite account so we can verify and delete it.

Email requests are typically processed within 3 to 5 business days.

What Happens to Your Data?

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following personal data is permanently wiped from our active databases (Supabase) and cannot be recovered:

  • Your Name, Email Address, and Phone Number.
  • Saved Delivery Addresses and saved location coordinates.
  • Your profile picture (if you are a driver).
  • Active notifications and session tokens.

Data That Is Retained

For legal, fraud-prevention, and accounting purposes, we are required by law to retain certain transactional data:

  • Financial Records: Anonymous receipts and payment transaction logs associated with completed orders will be retained for up to 7 years to comply with tax and accounting laws.
  • This retained data is completely anonymized or unlinked from your personal identity whenever legally possible.
