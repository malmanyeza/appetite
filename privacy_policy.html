Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Thank you for downloading and using Appetite ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Appetite mobile application (the "App") and the services provided through it.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Profile Information: Drivers may be asked to provide a profile picture. To do this, we request access to your device's Camera and Photo Library.
  • Delivery Information: When placing orders, you provide delivery addresses, special instructions, and location landmarks.
  • Payment Information: When you make purchases, we collect necessary payment information. Payments are securely processed through third-party payment gateways (e.g., Paynow, EcoCash). We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: To provide location-based services such as delivery tracking, restaurant distance calculation, and GPS capture for your address, we request access to your device's location data. You can enable or disable location tracking at any time through your device settings.
  • Device Information: We may collect device-specific information such as your hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect diagnostic data and crash reports to improve the performance and reliability of the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and maintain the App, including processing food orders and facilitating deliveries.
  • To manage your account and communicate with you regarding your orders, updates, and support inquiries.
  • To process payments securely.
  • To send you push notifications relating to your order status (e.g., restaurant accepted order, driver dispatched), promotions, and updates (subject to your notification preferences).
  • To monitor usage, detect technical errors, and improve the App's functionality.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following scenarios:

  • Service Providers: We share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our App. This includes cloud hosting providers (e.g., Supabase) and payment processors (e.g., Paynow).
  • Restaurants and Drivers: We share necessary details (name, delivery address, phone number) with the restaurant fulfilling your order and the driver assigned to deliver it, strictly for the purpose of completing your order.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Your Right to Delete Data: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and associated personal data, you may do so through your account settings inside the App or by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
